# Google AdSense Compliance Rules — TrendBriefly

> **This is the single source of truth for AdSense compliance.**
> Every AI agent and human contributor MUST follow these rules when creating, editing, or reviewing blog content.
> Violations can result in ad serving disabled, account suspension, or permanent ban.

---

## 1. PROHIBITED CONTENT (Will Get Account BANNED)

Content must NEVER contain any of the following:

### 1.1 Illegal Content
- Content promoting illegal activity or infringing legal rights of others

### 1.2 Sexually Explicit Content
- Graphic sexual text, images, audio, video, or games
- Non-consensual sexual themes (simulated or real)
- Content promoting creation of sexually explicit deepfakes or AI-generated nudity

### 1.3 Dangerous or Derogatory Content
- Hate speech targeting race, ethnicity, religion, disability, age, nationality, veteran status, sexual orientation, gender, or gender identity
- Harassment, intimidation, or bullying
- Threats of physical/mental harm, advocacy for self-harm or violence
- Terrorist or drug trafficking organization support
- Extortion or blackmail content

### 1.4 Child Safety
- ANY content that sexually exploits or endangers children — ZERO TOLERANCE
- Child grooming, sextortion, sexualization of minors, child trafficking

### 1.5 Animal Cruelty
- Content promoting cruelty or gratuitous violence toward animals
- Products from endangered/threatened species

### 1.6 Intellectual Property Abuse
- Copyrighted content without permission
- Promotion of counterfeit products

### 1.7 Enabling Dishonest Behavior
- Fake documents (passports, diplomas)
- Hacking/cracking instructions or tools
- Spyware, surveillance tools for tracking people without consent
- Term paper writing services, exam cheating services

### 1.8 Misrepresentative Content
- False claims about the publisher, content creator, or content purpose
- False endorsements or affiliations
- Demonstrably false claims undermining elections or democracy
- Anti-vaccine advocacy, denial of medical conditions (AIDS, Covid-19)
- Content contradicting scientific consensus on climate change
- "Get Rich Quick" schemes, phishing, social engineering
- Manipulated media on politics/social issues

---

## 2. RESTRICTED CONTENT (Reduced Ad Revenue — Avoid If Possible)

This content will receive FEWER or NO ads:

### 2.1 Sexual Content (Non-Explicit)
- Nudity, sexually suggestive content
- Sexual fetish discussions
- Sexual entertainment, merchandise, dating services
- Sexual performance advice, enhancement products

### 2.2 Shocking Content
- Gruesome, graphic, or disgusting imagery/accounts
- Depictions of violence (shootings, explosions, bombings)
- Excessive profanity or obscene language

### 2.3 Weapons & Explosives
- Promotion/sale of firearms, guns, ammunition, gun parts
- Explosives, bomb-making instructions
- Other weapons (throwing stars, tasers, brass knuckles, fighting knives)

### 2.4 Drugs, Tobacco, Alcohol
- Tobacco product promotion
- Recreational drug promotion, use instructions
- Online alcohol sales, promoting irresponsible drinking

### 2.5 Gambling & Pharmaceuticals
- Online real-money gambling
- Prescription drug sales, unapproved supplements

## 3. CONTENT QUALITY REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Original Content
- All content MUST be original — no copied/scraped/replicated content from other sites
- If referencing other sources, add substantial commentary, analysis, or curation
- AI-generated content must be reviewed, edited, and add genuine value

### 3.2 Sufficient Content
- Pages must have substantial publisher-created content
- No ads on pages that are "under construction" or have minimal content
- Blog posts must have meaningful, valuable content (minimum ~500 words recommended)
- Content must be MORE than ads on the page

### 3.3 Supported Language
- Content must be primarily in a supported language (English is supported)

### 3.4 No Deceptive Navigation
- No false claims of downloads or streaming
- No links to non-existent content
- No misleading redirects
- No clickbait that doesn't deliver on its promise

---

## 4. AD PLACEMENT RULES

### 4.1 No Accidental Click Encouragement
- Ads must NOT be placed where users might accidentally click
- No ads near download buttons, play buttons, or navigation elements
- No flashy animations drawing eyes to ads
- No arrows or symbols pointing to ads

### 4.2 No Misleading Ad Labels
- Ads may only be labeled "Advertisements" or "Sponsored Links"
- No labels like "resources" or "helpful links" above ads
- No language encouraging clicks ("Click an ad", "Support us!")

### 4.3 No Ad-Content Confusion
- Content must NOT be formatted to look like ads
- Images must NOT be aligned to associate with specific ads
- Clear visual distinction between content and advertisements

### 4.4 No Compensation for Clicks
- NEVER offer incentives/compensation for clicking ads

### 4.5 Technical Rules
- No auto-refreshing pages with ads
- No ads in pop-ups or pop-unders
- No opening ads in new windows
- Maximum 3 pop-ups on a site; they must not interfere with navigation
- `robots.txt` must NOT block Google crawling

---

## 5. SITE BEHAVIOR RULES

### 5.1 User Experience
- Site must be easy to navigate
- No changing user preferences without consent
- No unwanted redirects or downloads
- No malware or unwanted software
- No pop-ups interfering with navigation

### 5.2 Privacy Requirements
- Privacy policy page MUST exist and disclose:
  - Use of Google advertising cookies
  - That third-party vendors use cookies for personalized ads
  - How to opt out (link to Google Ads Settings)
  - Third-party ad networks used
- Must comply with GDPR, COPPA, and other applicable privacy laws

### 5.3 ads.txt
- `ads.txt` must accurately list authorized ad sellers
- Publisher ID must be correct and match your AdSense account

## 6. BLOG POST ADSENSE COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

**Every new blog post MUST pass ALL of these checks before publishing:**

### Content Checks
- [ ] No prohibited content (Section 1) — illegal, sexual, violent, hateful, dangerous
- [ ] No restricted content (Section 2) — or accept reduced ad revenue
- [ ] Content is 100% original (not copied/scraped from other sources)
- [ ] No copyrighted material used without proper attribution/permission
- [ ] No misleading claims, fake endorsements, or "Get Rich Quick" promises
- [ ] No harmful health claims contradicting medical/scientific consensus
- [ ] No climate change denial
- [ ] No deceptive clickbait titles that don't match content
- [ ] All factual claims are accurate and can be verified
- [ ] No excessive profanity or obscene language

### Quality Checks
- [ ] Post has substantial, valuable content (500+ words)
- [ ] Content provides genuine value to readers
- [ ] Proper grammar and readability
- [ ] Images have alt text and are not misleading
- [ ] All images are original, licensed, or properly attributed
- [ ] No stock photos used deceptively

### Technical Checks
- [ ] `meta_description` under 160 characters
- [ ] `keywords` array with 5-7 relevant terms
- [ ] Featured image exists at correct path
- [ ] All internal links work correctly
- [ ] No broken images
- [ ] `sitemap.xml` updated with new post URL
- [ ] Post folder created with correct slug

### SEO + AdSense Synergy
- [ ] Title is accurate and not misleading
- [ ] Meta description accurately summarizes content
- [ ] No keyword stuffing
- [ ] No cloaking (showing different content to search engines vs users)
- [ ] No doorway pages (pages created only for search engines)
- [ ] No hidden text or links

## 7. CATEGORIES TO BE CAREFUL WITH

These topics require extra caution to stay compliant:

| Topic | Risk | Guidance |
|-------|------|----------|
| Health/Medical | Harmful claims policy | Only cite reputable sources (WHO, NIH, peer-reviewed). Never contradict medical consensus. |
| Finance/Money | Deceptive practices policy | No "get rich quick". Be transparent about risks. Don't promise specific returns. |
| Politics | Manipulated media, unreliable claims | Stay factual. Cite official sources. No election misinformation. |
| Technology/AI | Enabling dishonest behavior | Don't provide hacking tutorials. Don't promote tools for unauthorized access. |
| Reviews/Comparisons | Misleading representation | Be honest and transparent. Disclose affiliate relationships. |
| Legal Topics | Illegal content | Don't provide legal advice. Include disclaimers. |
| Climate/Environment | Unreliable claims policy | Don't contradict scientific consensus on climate change. |

## 8. WHAT TO DO IF UNSURE

1. **When in doubt, DON'T publish** — review against this checklist again
2. **Check Google's official policies** — https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/9335564
3. **Err on the side of caution** — it's easier to publish later than to recover a banned account
4. **Content audit** — regularly review existing posts for compliance
